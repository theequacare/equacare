About Equacare LLC

Your trusted partner for non-medical home care services

Our Story

Founded with a passion for helping others, Equacare LLC specializes in non-medical home care services for families in our community. We focus on personal care assistance, companionship, and daily living support - not medical treatments or nursing care.

Our non-medical caregivers help with everyday activities like bathing, dressing, meal preparation, light housekeeping, and providing meaningful companionship. We believe that everyone deserves to maintain their independence and dignity while receiving the personal care assistance they need at home.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, personalized, and professional home care services that enhance the quality of life and promote independence for our clients while giving families peace of mind.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected home care provider in our community, known for our commitment to excellence, compassion, and innovative care solutions.

Our Values

  • Compassion and Empathy
  • Respect and Dignity
  • Excellence in Care
  • Integrity and Trust
  • Client-Centered Approach
