Non-Medical Home Care Services

Personal care, companionship, and daily living assistance - no medical procedures

How We Can Help

At Equacare LLC, we specialize exclusively in non-medical home care services. We do not provide medical treatments, nursing care, or clinical services. Instead, we focus on personal care assistance, companionship, and help with daily living activities to support your loved ones' independence and well-being at home.

Homemaking

  • Meal preparation
  • Cleaning
  • Laundry
  • Making beds
  • Changing light bulbs
  • and more!

Aide Services

  • Medication reminders
  • Health monitoring
  • Adherence to special diets
  • Help walking and mobility assistance
  • Skin care
  • Bathing assistance
  • Grooming
  • Dressing
  • Feeding
  • and more!

Companionship

  • Help with remote controls
  • Getting the mail
  • Running errands
  • Help with correspondence: letters, emails, social media
  • Playing games and helping with hobbies
  • Supervision when there is a risk of wandering or self-injury
  • and more!
