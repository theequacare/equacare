Non-Medical Home Care Services

At Equacare LLC, we specialize exclusively in non-medical home care services. We do not provide medical treatments, nursing care, or clinical services. Instead, we focus on personal care assistance, companionship, and help with daily living activities to support your loved ones' independence and well-being at home.

Our non-medical services include: personal care assistance, companion care, meal preparation, light housekeeping, transportation, medication reminders (not administration), and more.

Personal Care Assistance

Our caregivers provide compassionate assistance with activities of daily living including bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, and personal hygiene. We help maintain dignity while ensuring comfort and safety.

Companion Care

More than just assistance, we provide meaningful companionship. Our caregivers engage in conversation, play games, participate in hobbies, and provide the social interaction that enriches daily life and prevents isolation.

Meal Planning & Preparation

Nutritious, delicious meals tailored to dietary requirements and preferences. We plan menus, shop for groceries, prepare meals, and provide assistance with eating when needed to ensure proper nutrition.

Light Housekeeping

Maintaining a clean, organized home environment. Services include laundry, dishes, vacuuming, dusting, organizing, and general tidying to create a safe and comfortable living space.

Transportation Services

Safe, reliable transportation to medical appointments, grocery shopping, social events, religious services, and other errands. We help maintain independence and connection to the community.

Medication Reminders

Timely reminders to take medications as prescribed by healthcare providers. We help organize medications and ensure adherence to medication schedules for better health outcomes.

Mobility Assistance

Safe assistance with walking, transferring, and using mobility aids. We help prevent falls and maintain physical activity levels appropriate for each individual's abilities.

Overnight Care

Peace of mind through the night with our overnight care services. Caregivers provide supervision, assistance, and companionship during nighttime hours to ensure safety and comfort.

Dementia & Alzheimer's Care

Specialized care for individuals with memory impairments. Our trained caregivers provide patient, understanding support while engaging clients in appropriate activities and ensuring their safety.

Respite Care

Temporary relief for family caregivers. Whether you need a few hours or several days, our caregivers step in to provide quality care so you can rest, recharge, or attend to other responsibilities.

Post-Hospitalization Care

Transitional care following hospital discharge. We help with recovery, follow medical care plans, assist with exercises, and ensure a smooth, safe return to home life.

Flexible Scheduling

Care when you need it - hourly, daily, overnight, or live-in care options. We work with your schedule and can adjust care plans as needs change over time.
